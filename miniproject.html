HISTORICAL PLACES IN INDIA

“India,” the most explored, loved, charming, and oldest civilization in the world is the home to distinct cultures and rituals, languages, food, states, and cities. The country is also popular for its huge population in the world, and it comes in second place after China. It is popularly known as having the second-largest population in the world. And besides having the second-largest population and largest democracy in the world, the country of colourful cultures has seen many governments, dynasties, and rulers, owing to which the real historical places, cultural values, and the identity of India were affected and destroyed. Empires who ruled India and damaged its ethical values left no stone unturned in inventing their symbols to mark their ruling era by establishing various monuments. Various historical places in India, established under the rule of invaders and dynasties, exhibit what our culture, traditions, values, and people went through. You must read about and visit several historical places established there to know India better. Here in this article, we will describe various magnificent places that remind us of India’s culture and nature, some of which witnessed the bloodshed, pain, cruelty, and encroachment on Indian lands.

1.TAJ MAHAL

Taj Mahal is believed to symbolize love as Shahjahan( the Mughal emperor) established this mesmerizing white marble monument for his beautiful and darling wife. It is one of the most popular and charming places to visit in Agra, and the visit to the Taj Mahal will also make you learn and explore the Mughal era in India. The historical monument was built in the 17th century, surrounded by big beautiful gardens with various flowers, orchids, and fragrances. The best time to visit this elegant Mughal-built monument is in September, October, November, December, January, February, and March, as the weather is pleasant, comfortable, and far from heat and humidity. To enter the Taj Mahal, you will have to pay Rs 50 for Indian citizens and make sure you enter 30 minutes before sunrise and sunset, respectively. For foreign tourists, the entry fee is Rs 1100. There will be no fees for children who are under 15. The Taj Mahal comes from the top list of popular monuments in India.
2.QUTUB MINAR
The main aim of building Qutub Minar was to show the sign of victory of dominating the capital city of India by the Mughal emperor. The historic monument was built in 1193 and is considered the highest tower in India. It is one of the best Delhi Tourist Places that captivate people because of its historical values, ethics, cultures, and traditions. On weekends, you can visit the place and have fun and entertainment with your loved ones to spend quality time. The red-stone-built monument was established by Qutub-ud-din Aibak(the first Muslim ruler of Delhi) to symbolize the triumph of ruling and taking over Delhi. Qutub Minar is among the most spectacular monuments in India.
3.INDIA GATE
India Gate is the popular war memorial situated on Rajpath in Delhi. And this reputed monument is also known as the All-India War Memorial, and the India gate was established to dedicate troops of British India who died in wars, which were fought between 1914 and 1919. The monument was built in elegant and beautiful architecture, and it is considered the most reputed monument in India that exhibits the courage and scarifier of Indian troops.
4.DELHI IRON PILLER
The iron pillar is among the most magnificent monuments in India, and it is quite famous for its non-rusting state. And besides this fact, the long and breathtaking monument in India is believed to be built 98% from iron, and it was made using a forge or fire welding. The pillar is one of India’s most famous historical monuments, and it has become a major tourist attraction on vacation. The long-standing iron pillar was constructed to commemorate the Gupta period’s king.
5.CHARMINAR
Charminar is among India’s most popular historical monuments, and it is quite historic among tourists who visit India and are amazed by the country’s beauty and fascinating historical facts. Charminar’s glorious and breathtaking architecture makes it one of India’s most beautiful and historic monuments. The monument is one of the most famous buildings in Hyderabad, and it is usually visited by locals and tourists owing to its historical significance. Charminar is one of the historical monuments of India, and it is situated near Laad Bazaar and Makkah Masjid.
6.STATUE OF UNITY
“Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel Statue” is the world’s tallest statue, with a height of 182 meters. It took around 3000 crores to build this Sardar Patel Statue. It is located At Sardar Sarovar Dam, Unity Rd, Kevadia, Gujarat. Vadodara is considered the nearest city to the statue, 80-90 kilometers from the Unity statue. To reach “the statue of unity,” you can approach Vadodara and easily visit the place. And around 10000 – 15000 people come here to visit the place. “The Statue of Unity,” dedicated to Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, is the perfect example of integrity in diversity. The man is responsible for bringing Indians together against British rule in India. The monument is one of the old monuments in India, and it is Gujarat’s most respected place to visit on holidays.
